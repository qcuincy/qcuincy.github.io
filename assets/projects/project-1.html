Project 1

Benchmarking productivity.

1. Project Overview: This section provides an overview of the project and highlights my key contributions. For this collaborative project, our team was tasked with tackling a challenging problem that involved analyzing Github repository logs data. The main objective was to develop models capable of predicting lines of code added per author on a timely basis, providing valuable insights into the development process of software teams. As part of the team, my contributions played a pivotal role in driving progress and finding innovative solutions to the challenges we encountered. As the project lead, I actively facilitated group discussions and encouraged open dialogue, ensuring effective communication within the team. Additionally, I took charge of developing and coding the random walk model, conducting in-depth research to implement it efficiently.

2. Data Analysis and Visualization: In this section, I outline my responsibilities in data analysis and visualization. I played a vital role in data collection and created scripts to extract essential information from the provided dataset. Moreover, I designed various functions to calculate important metrics, such as lines per author per week. I also generated insightful plots, showcasing the distinct phases of our short and long-term models, and their average lines per author.

3. Model Development: This section highlights my involvement in model development for the project. I led the creation and coding of the random walk model, which involved researching how random walk models work and adapting them to address our specific problem. This contribution significantly enriched the analytical depth of our project. I chose to adapt the generic random walk model in order to encompass the true probabilities found in the data. To do this I had to calculate the probability of lines per author decreasing or increasing each week, and determine appropriate step sizes using an average change in lines per author per week.

4. Reflection and Learning: In this final section, I reflect on the project's progress and the challenges we faced. I emphasize the importance of effective communication and clear goal-setting in overcoming obstacles and achieving successful outcomes. Additionally, I acknowledge the lessons learned, such as maintaining steady progress and capitalizing on time advantages for future projects.
